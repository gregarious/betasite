About NOW

Right now you're on the NOW page, which gives you an at-a-glance view of the most recent and popular things going on in Oakland.

At the top you'll see a map showing where all the NOW items are located. Next to and below it are the most recent items from all of the other pages in the Oakland Scene:

  • Upcoming Events shows you what events are coming up.
  • Top Specials shows you the most popular specials being offered by Oakland businesses.
  • Chatterbox shows you the most recent interactions in the Chatter section.
  • News shows you the most recent articles about Oakland from local media sources.

Click on any of the items to get more details and click on the header of each section to see a full listing of events, specials, chatter or news.

Don't Forget...

you can always filter the results on the NOW page by using the filter box above Upcoming Events.
