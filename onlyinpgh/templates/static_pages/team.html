The Scenable Team

Brett Weiwiora

CEO

Brett Wiewiora is the founder of OnlyinPgh, Scenable’s parent company, and completed his Masters of Public Policy and Management at Carnegie Mellon’s Heinz College. A Pittsburgh native, Brett is an unabashed rust belt booster and a firm believer in the power of small, motivated groups of people to change the world.

Aside from bootstrapping startups, Brett’s interests include biking, gardening, history, community development, local economic policy and hugging trees. Feel free to contact Brett anytime by emailing brett@scenable.com.

Greg Nicholas

Lead Platform Architect

Greg Nicholas is a software developer born and raised in Pittsburgh. He graduated with a B.S. in Computer Science, Mathematics, and Psychology from the University of Pittsburgh in 2007 and earned his M.S. in Computer Science at Brown University in 2009. Greg’s interests include music, film, cooking, and running, and he’s happy to be working on Scenable to help connect the local communities he is a part of. He is a lumberjack and he’s okay, he sleeps all night and he works all day. Feel free to contact Greg anytime by emailing greg@scenable.com.

Lara Schenck

Lead UI/UX Designer and Developer

Lara grew up on farm Southeast of Pittsburgh. She has a B.F.A. in Digital Art from the School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston. She is also a skier, coffee enthusiast, and the creator of WPFolio Two, the WordPress theme for visual artists.

After Scenable is wildly successful, Lara’s future plans include attending CMU’s graduate program at the Entertainment Technology Center and starting a business designing games for college curricula (lectures and textbooks aren’t cutting it). Feel free to get in touch with Lara anytime by emailing lara@scenable.com.
