About Specials

Say hello to the Specials page which gives you a listing of all the fantastic specials offered by businesses in Oakland.

Check the map at the top to see where all the specials currently on the page are located. To see additional specials, simply hit the "see more" button at the bottom of the page.

To grab a special or to get more information about it, click on its map marker or on one of those listed.

About Scene Cred

Every time you purchase a special, you'll receive Scene Cred equal to the amount of the purchase. To see how much Scene Cred you have, look at the top right corner next to your user name. The more Scene Cred you have, the more active you are in your Scene.

Don't Forget...

You can always filter the results on the Specials page by using the filter box above the map.
