About Single Events

Now you're looking at a Single Event Page, which gives you more information about an particular event in Oakland.

At the top you'll see a map showing where the event is happening. Look next to and below it for more info.

Plan on going to the event? Add it to your favorites by clicking the icon under the map.

About Scene Cred

BONUS: Get Scene Cred for favoriting an event!

To see how much Scene Cred you have, look at the top right corner next to your user name. The the more active you are in your Scene the more Scene Cred you have and the more perks you'll get as Scenable develops.

Don't Forget...

Want to search the entire Oakland Scene for something? Use the search box in the top right corner of the page.
